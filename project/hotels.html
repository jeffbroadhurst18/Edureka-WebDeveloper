New York - San Carlos Hotel

The San Carlos Hotel brings spacious, apartment-style living and personalized service to the heart of Midtown Manhattan. With room sizes starting at 350 square feet and home-style conveniences like free Wi-Fi and kitchenettes with coffeemakers, mini fridges and microwaves, we're the top choice among NYC hotels for business travelers, couples and families. Stepping past our elegant, pre-war façade brings you into bright, contemporary luxury, where you will be warmly greeted by our friendly, attentive staff. Our boutique hotel features 147 sophisticated rooms and suites, from well-appointed Deluxe Rooms to Elite One Bedroom Suites with wet bars, and Penthouse Suites with private terraces overlooking the NYC cityscape. After a day of travel, meetings or exploring Manhattan, unwind with a workout in the fitness center. Savor a contemporary dining experience infused with the flavors of India and China at the Spice Symphony restaurant. For intimate business meetings and private events, the San Carlos Hotel features three distinctive function rooms highlighted by our Garden Terrace Penthouse, which features inspiring skyline views. Guests also enjoy access to our Business Lounge with six computers and two printers. When visiting NYC for work or play, the San Carlos Hotel invites you to relax in one of our 147 guest rooms and suites offering spacious layouts, luxury amenities, convenient kitchenettes with microwaves and mini fridges and coffeemakers and room service from Spice Symphony restaurant. Reflecting the modern style and energy of our Midtown Manhattan surroundings, all accommodations offer elegant retreats steeped in contemporary comfort. From 350-square-foot Deluxe Rooms appointed with Wi-Fi, LED televisions, docking stations and Corby pants presses to expansive Suites ranging from 400 to 600 square feet to palatial Penthouses with rooftop terraces, our accommodations combine apartment-style convenience with all the comforts you expect from a luxury boutique hotel.

Reykjavik - Hotel Plaza

At hotel Plaza you will find 201 bright and comfortable rooms and a spacious lobby area with hotel bar where you can relax after an eventful day in Reykjavik. Fully equipped meeting and conference facilities are available at Plaza. The hotel is ideal for business and leisure. Breakfast buffet is included and all rooms are equipped with a mini bar, telephone, flat screen TV, shower, hairdryer and wi-fi. The meeting room at CenterHotel Plaza has a luxurious and warm feel and is well equipped with the latest technology for a first class meeting or conference. The venues are bright and welcoming, located on the ground floor of CenterHotel Plaza. It covers 150 m² and can be subdivided into smaller sections of 3 x 50 m². The Venue opens up into a secluded patio garden, called Eldgjá – Firecanyon, where guests can take in the fresh air. Plaza Bar, located in the lobby lounge area is the prefect starting point for a memorable night out in Reykjavik. Designed to provide our guests with a comfortable and elegant setting. We have Happy Hour at the Plaza bar everyday from 16:00 – 18:00. Be sure to ask our staff about the offers we have during Happy hour.
